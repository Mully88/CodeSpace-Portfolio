Welcome to MK-Time

At MK-Time, time is more than a measure — it is an art form. Founded on the belief that precision and beauty should coexist seamlessly, we design our luxury timepieces in the heart of Switzerland, the world’s cradle of horological excellence. Every sketch, curve, and complication is conceived with meticulous attention to detail, inspired by centuries of watchmaking tradition.
Once perfected, our designs travel to Edinburgh, where innovation meets craftsmanship. In our Scottish workshops, master artisans bring each watch to life, combining cutting-edge manufacturing techniques with hand-finished artistry. This unique dual heritage — Swiss design and Scottish manufacture — ensures that every MK-Time watch embodies both timeless elegance and modern resilience.
Our philosophy is simple: to create watches that are not only worn, but experienced. Each piece reflects a commitment to authenticity, precision, and enduring style. Whether gracing a boardroom, a gala, or a quiet moment of reflection, an MK-Time watch is a companion for life’s most meaningful hours. At MK-Time, we don’t just keep time. We craft it.
